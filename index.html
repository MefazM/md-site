The Game

Magic Towers is a free-to-play mobile game with an innovative spell-casting system set in a fantasy world of warring mages. The game features real-time multiplayer tactical battles, city-building and strategy elements. Magic Towers provides a fresh blend of genres and new features combined with tried-and-true game mechanics.

Magic Towers is cross-platform, so you can play with all your friends, regardless of what kind of smartphone or tablet you have.

In the game you can battle both human and AI players, develop your home base (or “Shard”) just how you like it, build and upgrade beautifully-designed buildings and strong soldiers, and learn over 25 different powerful spells.
